Getting Used

To preface this post, on a social level I am quite naive. I don't understand social cues, I make jokes when they aren't appropriate, I'm a pushover and I hate the feeling of not being good enough for others ("Social Validation").

School has recently started for me and I've felt kinda down. It's been quite a struggle and the workload is pretty hard to balance but I make it through which is nice. The only reason I've stayed sane is through talking with my friends on Discord, usually listening to music or chatting in general and its become a really important part of my life.

I perform decently in school because I basically just no-life homework. In fact, at one point I was like 2 weeks ahead on school and I was still working. I guess you can say I'm a sweat/tryhard/nerd, but I do it just because I don't feel sound if I'm doing nothing. I've traded the fun of life I enjoyed previously for percieved "productivity"

Due to this, I needed something to balance out my life. That's why I turned to my friends, who were extremely supportive and I really had a great time. However, as the school year progressed on, in parallel with my no-life attitude to school, I feel I was being used by a partition of some who I felt closest to.

I'm not really sure how to write this - It really sucks when you feel like you're getting used and the only reason they talk to you is to ask for homework help or vent to you. It feels like you're just getting used yet you still really care about them. I guess a good analogy would be if your parents only used you to pay them money and otherwise they have no interaction with you.

"Ok... so why don't you just drop them? They just use you right? It's logical thing to do." I wish it was that easy - everytime they talk to me, I feel like they have good intentions and aren't trying to just use me, and I care about them too much not to help. However, any person that looked at my situation would see how bad it would be, but I really can't get out of it. I don't know what to do.
